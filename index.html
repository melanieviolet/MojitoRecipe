Mojito

A mojito is a refreshing mint cocktail

Ingredients

  • fresh mint
  • white rum
  • lime juice
  • white sugar or simple syrup
  • club soda or sparkling water
  • ice

Preparation

  1. Muddle the mint and a lime wedge in a cocktail shaker using a cocktail muddler
  2. Pour in the rum, syrup and rest of the lime juice and shake until well combined
  3. Strain the contents of the shaker into an ice-filled glass
  4. Fill the rest of the glass with your soda water
  5. Taste and adjust as needed
